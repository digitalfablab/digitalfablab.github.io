How it works:

Follow three simple steps and you'll be well on your way!

Schedule an appointment with us either by email or through the Google form located below

Chat with a representative during your appointment. We'll talk to you about your expectations, the project's technological requirements, and see if there's a good fit.

Sit back and be ready to provide feedback. We'll send you iteratively improved prototypes, so you'll be satisfied every time.

About us

The Fab Lab is the equivalent of an online maker space. We'll provide you with tools, server space, personel, and hardware so you can tackle any project of any scope. 80% of the small fee we charge goes directly towards the JHU student who is assisting you! You'll be saving money and supporting Hopkins developers simultaneously. The other 20% covers server and web hosting fees.
